Overview

This course will provide me with a unique opportunity to expand my understanding of how Microsoft Azure operates in both practical application and theoretical concepts. I look forward to immersing myself in various aspects of the Azure platform through a comprehensive curriculum designed to build my foundation in this field. Throughout the course, I will be introduced to fundamental elements of the Azure ecosystem, and I will have the opportunity to practically explore various services and tools that are offered. At the same time, I will also gain insight into the theoretical principles underlying the platform's functionality. This will help me understand not only what I am doing in practice, but also why it works the way it does.
